Design Notes

Calendar View: Day-by-day breakdown with drag-and-drop capability. Visual indicators for made recipes.
List View: Compact list with quick actions. Better for scanning all recipes at once.
Category View: Grouped by meal category for easy planning by type.
Mobile Optimizations: Swipe hints, bottom sheet actions, larger touch targets (44px min), stacked day view.
Progress Indicator: Visual progress bar showing completion status for the week.
Made Status: Recipes marked as made have reduced opacity, checkmark badge, and subtle overlay.
