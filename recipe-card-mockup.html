Design Enhancement Ideas

1. Image Placeholders

Add recipe image support with beautiful gradient placeholders or shimmer loading effects. Images make cards more visually appealing and help users quickly identify recipes.

2. Enhanced Visual Hierarchy

Better spacing, typography weights, and color contrast help users scan cards quickly. Consider larger titles, clearer tag styling, and more prominent favorite indicators.

3. Hover Effects

Smooth lift animations, shadow transitions, and scale effects create a premium feel. Cards should feel interactive and responsive.

4. Glass Morphism & Backdrop Blur

Modern glass effects with backdrop blur create depth and visual interest, especially when overlaying images or gradients.

5. Accent Colors & Borders

Side accent bars, colored borders, or gradient backgrounds can add personality while maintaining the warm terracotta & sage palette.

6. Better Tag Styling

Rounded tags with borders, subtle shadows, or icon badges make categories and tags more scannable and visually distinct.

7. Action Button Improvements

Color-coded buttons with icons, hover states, and better spacing make actions clearer. Consider icon-only on desktop, text labels on mobile.

8. Texture & Patterns

Subtle textures, dot patterns, or geometric overlays add visual interest without overwhelming the content.
